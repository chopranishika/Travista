About Us

Travista is an intelligent guide which connects a user to the city in its own unique way.

We provide great services and ideas

A user-friendly web application based upon tours and travels. Travista helps the tourists to explore the city’s local attractions and its surrounding along with the transportation options, accommodations and give updates as per the user’s requirements. Travista gives reviewed feedbacks and the places can be shared with peers as well. In this way it helps the users to organize a tour plan accordingly.

It is able to pick up user’s location and display results accordingly. Along with tourists it also helps local people conveniently with navigation, transportation, traffic updates, informing about best deals around them.
